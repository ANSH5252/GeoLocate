## Reporting a Vulnerability

We take the security of our Geospatial platform seriously. If you discover a vulnerability, please follow these steps:

1.  **Do not** open a public issue.
2.  Email the project lead at **[INSERT YOUR EMAIL HERE]** with the subject line "Security Vulnerability".
3.  Include details about the vulnerability and steps to reproduce it.
4.  We will respond within 48 hours.

Thank you for helping keep GeoLocate safe!
